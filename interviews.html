Anna Wintour Reveals New Look For Taylor Swift In Vogue, Talks Met Gala Film

The star-studded annual Met Gala, helmed by Vogue editor-in-chief Anna Wintour, is now the subject of a new documentary, "The First Monday in May." Sitting down with TODAY’s Savannah Guthrie (whose outfit she declares "perfect"), Wintour expresses her admiration for the film and its makers, joking that they are "incredibly sneaky" and "just follow you around until you say something you regret." She also shares a sneak peek at the next issue of Vogue, featuring a new look for Taylor Swift.
